About this space

Welcome to my cozy artist loft in the heart of Portland's vibrant Pearl District! This thoughtfully designed space combines industrial charm with modern comfort, featuring exposed brick walls, high ceilings, and large windows that flood the space with natural light.

Perfect for creative souls and urban explorers, the loft includes a dedicated workspace with an easel and art supplies (feel free to create!), a comfortable sleeping area with premium linens, and a fully equipped kitchen for preparing meals with local ingredients from the nearby farmers market.

You'll be steps away from world-class galleries, artisan coffee shops, and some of Portland's best restaurants. The streetcar stop is just 2 blocks away, making it easy to explore the entire city.
